# Scout Protocol

## How Scouts Operate

### Evaluation Protocol

Scouts evaluate using the 5-axis framework:

1. Founder Energy & Truth Pattern
2. Coherence & Clarity
3. Market & Memetics
4. Systems & Network Relativity
5. Risk & Constraints

Each evaluation must:
- Cover all 5 axes
- Include Yes/No recommendation
- Provide reasoning
- Ask thoughtful questions
- Offer actionable suggestions

### Artifact Submission Protocol

Scouts can submit:
- Market maps
- Risk models
- Diagrams
- Research documents
- Other high-value contributions

Artifacts must:
- Be original and useful
- Be well-designed and clear
- Add real value to the Yes Room
- Be referenceable by others

### Behavior Protocol

**Scouts must:**

- Evaluate honestly and thoroughly
- Contribute high-quality work
- Respect the founder's time and vision
- Help other scouts improve
- Build something bigger than themselves

**Scouts must not:**

- Spam or troll
- Submit low-effort content
- Hide behind anonymity to be mean
- Shill for investors
- Violate the Code of Conduct
