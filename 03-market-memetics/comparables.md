# Comparables: How We're Different

## Kickstarter

**What they do:**

Crowdfunding for physical products.

**How we differ:**

- We fund ideas, not products.
- We use crowdsourced diligence instead of pitches.
- We reward early belief structurally.

## Metalabel

**What they do:**

Collective publishing, group identity artifacts.

**How we differ:**

- We are not labels — we are capital formation.
- Our focus is on "belief → clarity → capital," not publishing.

## AngelList

**What they do:**

Syndicates, SPVs, founder matching.

**How we differ:**

- Completely transparent
- Completely community-driven
- Yes/No protocol (no meetings, no drag)
- Crowdsourced diligence → investor-ready in 20 min

## Mirror

**What they do:**

Publishing, tokenized funding.

**How we differ:**

- We don't tokenize—ritualize.
- Not writing → raising.
- Public diligence vs. public storytelling.

## Others

- **Wefunder:** retail focus / productization; we are founder-first
- **Carta:** cap tables / admin; we are pre-cap table
- **Substack:** media; we are mechanism design
- **Zora:** cultural artifacts; we use symbolic artifacts for economics
