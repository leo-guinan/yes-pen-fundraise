# Competitor Deep Dives

## Competitor 1: Kickstarter

**Strengths:** community, crowdfunding

**Weaknesses:** no diligence, no investor logic, not idea-stage

## Competitor 2: AngelList

**Strengths:** investor access

**Weaknesses:** opaque, slow, meeting-heavy

## Competitor 3: Wefunder

**Strengths:** public raises

**Weaknesses:** regulatory burdens, generic, no community diligence
