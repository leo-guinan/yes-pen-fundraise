# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

### Be Respectful

- Treat everyone with respect
- No harassment, discrimination, or abuse
- No personal attacks
- No trolling or spamming

### Be Constructive

- Criticism is fine, but be helpful
- Focus on ideas, not people
- Provide actionable feedback
- Help others improve

### Be Honest

- Don't shill for investors
- Don't hide truth
- Don't exaggerate or mislead
- Don't pretend to know what you don't

### Be Collaborative

- Help other scouts
- Improve templates
- Share knowledge
- Build something bigger than yourself

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or spamming
- Investor shilling
- Violating others' privacy
- Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations of the Code of Conduct may result in:

- Warning
- Temporary ban
- Permanent ban
- Removal of contributions

## Reporting

If you see violations, report them by:

- Opening an issue
- Contacting the founder directly
- Contacting the core team

**We're building something better. Let's act like it.**
